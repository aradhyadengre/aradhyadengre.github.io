Siddhi I Really Love You!

I feel immensely lucky to have you in my life. You are the only person with whom I can share anything and everything. It gives the impression to me that there was no life for me till I met you and got in love with you at once. Your heart.

The human head is a constantly moving and turning pedestal.

My Darling Siddhi, I never knew the meaning of true love till the day I met you and having you as my sweetheart is like a dream come true for me. I become a little weak when it comes to speak my feelings in front of you, so I have thought to pen down my feelings, as I did not want to miss a chance to let you know that you are an angel, my true companion, my best friend and my soulmate. I just wish to tell you that I love you till eternity. I am madly in love with your gentle smile, the spark in your eyes, your warm touch and essence of the bond that we share. I cherish each and every moment spent with you.

Siidhi I really Love you ~ Aradhya Dengre
